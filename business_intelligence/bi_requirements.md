## 2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
| KPI Name | Target | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Daily Revenue** | > 50,000 RWF | Daily |
| **Kitchen Wait Time** | < 15 Minutes | Real-time |
| **Dead Stock** | < 5 Items | Weekly |
